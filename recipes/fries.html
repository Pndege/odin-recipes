French Fries

Description
Fries, also known as French fries, are long, thin strips of potato that are deep-fried until golden brown and crispy, usually served hot and salted as a popular side dish or snack, often accompanied by ketchup or other dipping sauces; they can be cut in various styles like curly fries, steak fries, or shoestring fries depending on the desired texture

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Any seasoning of choice

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into even strips
  2. Soak them in cool water
  3. Switch on the stove and take a frying pan and put the oil required for frying and let the oil heat for a while
  4. Take out the potato sticks and fry them in the oil in simmer
  5. After you get a nice golden yellow colour, take them out and put in the tissue paper to get rid of excess oil.
