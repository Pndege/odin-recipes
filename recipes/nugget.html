Chicken Nuggets

Description
Chicken nuggets are bite-sized pieces of chicken that are coated with breading or batter and then fried or baked. They are a popular fast food item that are often served with dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh pieces, ground chicken, or other parts of the chicken
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Flour
  • eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Preservatives and fillers

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces
  2. Season the chicken
  3. Dredge the chicken in flour
  4. Dip the chicken in egg
  5. Dredge the chicken in breadcrumbs
  6. Fry or Bake the chicken
