Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description
Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid pasta that's a staple of Italian cuisine. The word spaghetti comes from the Italian word spaghetto, which means "little string"

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • Tomato sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Make the Meatball Mixture
  3. Form the Meatballs
  4. Brown the Meatballs
  5. Bake the Meatballs
  6. Prepare the Sauce
  7. Add Meatballs to Sauce
  8. Cook the Spaghetti
  9. Drain the Spaghetti and Serve
